## ğŸ½ï¸ Setting Up Your Menu

### Create Your First Menu

1. Click **"Menu"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Menu"** button
3. Give it a name (like "Lunch Menu" or "Main Menu")
4. Click Create

### Add Categories

Categories organize your menu (like "Appetizers", "Main Courses", "Desserts")

1. In your menu, click **"Add Category"**
2. Enter category name
3. Click Save

### Add Menu Items

1. Click on a category
2. Click **"Add Item"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Item name** - "Grilled Salmon"
   - **Description** - "Fresh Atlantic salmon with lemon butter"
   - **Price** - Enter in dollars (like 24.95)
   - **Preparation time** - How long to cook (minutes)
   - **Tags** - vegetarian, vegan, halal, spicy (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**

Repeat for all your menu items!

### Mark Items Unavailable (Sold Out)

**When you run out of ingredients:**

1. Go to **"Menu"**
2. Find the item
3. Click the **toggle switch** next to it (turns red/grey)
4. Item is now hidden from customers

**Back in stock?** Just toggle it back on! (turns green)

## ğŸª‘ Setting Up Your Tables

### Add Your Tables

1. Click **"Tables"** (or ask admin to add tables for you)
2. For each table, you need:
   - **Table number** - "1", "2", "Table A", etc.
   - **Capacity** - How many people fit
   - **Location** (optional) - "By window", "Patio", etc.

Example:
- Table 1, Capacity 2
- Table 2, Capacity 4
- Table 3, Capacity 6
- Table 4, Capacity 8

### Check Table Availability

1. Click **"Reservations"** or **"Tables"**
2. Pick a date and time
3. See how many tables are available
4. The system automatically assigns the best table

**Example:** If someone books for 4 people at 7 PM, the system finds a table that:
- Seats 4 or more people
- Is free at 7 PM
- Automatically reserves it for 2 hours
